About Me

I am a recovering attorney. That might seem like a slap in the face to other attorneys, but I don't mean it that way. I applaud my attorney colleagues who make it work. In my case, private practice was something that consumed me in a very negative way. It was something I needed to leave behind.

On the other hand, I enjoy developing. I really do. I've never worked for a company as any type of developer in job title. In fact, it's been years since I've worked for a company in any technology capacity. But I always end up finding a problem that needs a solution. And web apps have become my flavor for solutions.

Let's connect and see what marvelous things we can build together.
